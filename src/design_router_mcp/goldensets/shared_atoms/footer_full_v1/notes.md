Every page must ship with a footer. Do not skip it.

This footer has 3 columns: brand + tagline, service area links, and a contact block with phone, address, and hours.

Adopt this structure directly. Change the business name, phone, address, hours, and service links to match the brief.

The footer sits on a subtle border-top separator. Keep the type muted and smaller than body copy. The phone number should be a tappable `tel:` link on mobile.

Do not build a one-line stub footer. The footer should feel like it belongs to the same page as the hero.
