How we work

Three ways to engage

Get the scope right first

A short, structured kickoff to map goals, constraints, and the work that actually matters before anything is built.

  • Goals + success measures defined together
  • Clear written summary you can act on
  • Honest read on timeline and fit

Steady, checkpoint-based delivery

Work moves in reviewable checkpoints so direction stays correctable and nothing drifts for weeks unseen.

  • Reviewable progress at each checkpoint
  • Scope adjustable between checkpoints
  • One point of contact throughout

Help after launch

Ongoing maintenance and small improvements on a predictable cadence, so the work keeps earning its keep.

  • Predictable monthly cadence
  • Priority queue for fixes
  • Quarterly review of what's next
