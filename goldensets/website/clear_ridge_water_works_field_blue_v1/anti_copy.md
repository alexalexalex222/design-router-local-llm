# Clear Ridge Water Works — Anti-Copy & Source Hygiene

This pack is **synthetic** visual grammar for routing frontend-weak LLMs toward a premium rural field-service layout. It is not a real company. Borrow the **diagnostic structure and interaction discipline only** — never the literal brand, copy, or any claim.

## Do not reuse / copy
- The **"Clear Ridge Water Works"** name, the mark, the exact navigation labels, the exact headline ("What's happening with your water?"), or the exact section copy one-to-one.
- The specific **token values** (the exact field-blue/cyan/limestone hex set), the exact diagram geometry, or the exact plan names ("Seasonal Check / System Care / Filtration Watch") as if they were a real catalog.
- The diagnostic readouts as **engineering advice**. They are plausible field grammar for design context — not a service manual, code reference, or repair instruction.

## Forbidden patterns (zero tolerance — reject the output if present)
Never invent, and never carry forward from this pack as if true:
- A **license / certification / registration number**, or any "licensed / bonded / insured" claim.
- A **response time** ("24/7", "same-day guaranteed", "30-minute"), an **uptime/SLA**, or any timing promise. Use "ask about current availability."
- **Service towns, cities, counties, ZIP codes, or map pins.** Coverage is a region *concept* only; real towns go in `[SERVICE_AREA_TOWNS]` *after confirmation*.
- **Reviews, ratings, star counts, testimonials, customer names, job counts, "X years in business," awards, partner logos, or manufacturer endorsements.**
- A **guarantee, warranty length, discount, coupon, or price** stated as fact. Plans describe scope, not fixed pricing.
- **Phone numbers / emails / addresses** as real — use `[PHONE_NUMBER]`, `[EMAIL_ADDRESS]`.
- Beautiful **fake trust**: filler trust badges, invented "as seen in," or a fabricated service record. The record area ships labeled placeholders (`[SERVICE_TYPE]`, `[DATE]`, `[OUTCOME]`) only.

## Source / asset hygiene
- **No external assets**: no external fonts/CDN/JS, no `<img>`, no CSS `url()` / `background-image`, no external image URLs, no data-URI image blobs, no route screenshot paths baked into the page.
- **No emoji as icons.** All iconography and illustration is bespoke inline SVG — one SVG, one job; nothing recycled as filler.
- Keep `screenshot_paths` empty in the manifest until a conductor captures real renders.

## Routing hygiene (don't over-trigger)
- Anchor for **rural / trade field-service** verticals (well/water/pump/filtration/septic-adjacent, and structurally similar diagnostic trades). Do **not** soften into generic SaaS dashboards, marketing-agency hero gradients, wellness/spa, or a corporate brochure.
- Don't collapse the **plan comparison** into a generic three-equal-card row — its asymmetry (size + elevation + the dark featured plan) is the point.
- Don't drop the **proof-safe** framing when reusing atoms; the honesty *is* the brand.
