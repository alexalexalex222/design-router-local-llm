# FreezeBreeze Live Lab — Anti-Copy & Source Hygiene

This pack is **synthetic visual grammar**. Borrow the structure, motion, and proof discipline — never the literal content.

## Do not reuse

- The **FreezeBreeze Live Lab** brand name, the frost-mark logo, the exact navigation labels, or the exact section copy.
- The exact ice-blue / navy / cyan token *values*, the exact headline wording ("See the airflow before you decide."), the exact timecodes, segment names, or script talk-track lines.
- The specific schematic geometry, the heat-map gradient stops, the cue-state labels, or the FAQ question/answer wording one-to-one. Reconstruct the *idea* (a drawn product schematic, an honest objection FAQ), not the pixels.

## Forbidden patterns (zero tolerance)

- **No fake proof of any kind:** no reviews, ratings, testimonials, star counts, "units sold", live-viewer counts, follower numbers, influencer or celebrity endorsements, award badges, or media-outlet logos.
- **No fabricated commerce pressure:** no percentage discounts, no "was/now" pricing, no countdown timers, no "only N left", no manufactured scarcity or urgency.
- **No invented performance claims:** no temperature drops, coverage areas, decibel levels, energy figures, or "best/#1/guaranteed" language unless the operator supplies verified, sourced evidence.
- **No fake product media:** no `<img>`, no raster `url()`/`background-image`, no data-URI image blobs, no external image/video/CDN URLs, no external fonts. The product is drawn in CSS/SVG only.
- **No emoji as icons.** Bespoke inline SVG only, one SVG per job.

## Source hygiene

- Every claimable value ships as a labelled placeholder (`[PRICE — confirm before publishing]`, `[DATE — confirm before publishing]`, `[REACH_FIGURE — confirm with verified testing]`, `[NOISE_LEVEL — confirm with verified testing]`, `[RETURN_POLICY — confirm before publishing]`, `[SUPPORT_CONTACT — confirm before publishing]`, `[OFFER_DETAIL — confirm before publishing]`, `[CLIP — add verified demo footage]`).
- The schematic and heat map are **explicitly labelled as illustrative teaching aids**; never present them as measured data.
- Proof-safe filler only — e.g. "Add verified demo clips here.", "Queue for review before publishing.", "Ask about current availability." Do not replace placeholders with guesses; replace them only with operator-supplied verified facts.

## Routing hygiene

Do not let this pack bleed into unrelated verticals. It is a **live-commerce / product-education** anchor. Keep it away from fight-gym, legal, garden-care, medspa, and SaaS-dashboard grammar. If the target is a different physical product, keep the broadcast structure and the proof discipline but redraw the schematic and re-tone the palette to fit that product.
