How it works

Three things to understand before the cold makes sense.

Product education first. These callouts give viewers a mental model so the live demo lands — without overstating what the unit does.

1 — Air has to move

A cooling unit works by moving air across a chilled surface. The fan and the path it takes matter as much as the cold itself.

2 — Reach is a zone, not a number

Cool air spreads and fades with distance. We show the comfort zone on camera instead of promising a single figure.

3 — Settings change the feel

Speed, oscillation, and quiet mode each trade something. The demo lets viewers judge the trade for their own space.

Objection handling

The questions that come up mid-stream.

Honest answers to the doubts buyers raise on air. Where a real figure belongs, there is a placeholder — not a guess.

Will this actually cool my room?

It moves cooled air through a defined comfort zone rather than chilling an entire room like an AC unit. The live demo shows that zone honestly. Match it to your space and expectations.

How far does the air reach?

Reach is a zone that fades with distance, not a single number. [REACH_FIGURE — confirm with verified testing] before stating a measurement on air.

Is it loud?

There is a quiet mode that trades some airflow for lower noise. We run it live so you can hear it for yourself. [NOISE_LEVEL — confirm with verified testing] before quoting a figure.
