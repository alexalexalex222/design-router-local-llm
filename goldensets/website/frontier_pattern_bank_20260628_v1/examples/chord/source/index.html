About the data. Both datasets are illustrative samples built to be structurally realistic — directional, asymmetric, with believable corridors — but the numbers are synthetic and not authoritative measurements. Use them to read the shape of a relationship matrix, not to cite a figure. Built with d3-chord · chord(), chordDirected(), ribbon(), ribbonArrow(), arc().
