About this report

The Meridian is a fictional publication. Every figure here is computed from a synthetic, seeded dataset of 120,000 reading events generated in your browser — no real people, publishers, or numbers. It exists to demonstrate a coordinated cross-filter, where brushing one chart re-aggregates them all.

Method

Events span a 90-day edition with an engineered upward trend and weekly rhythm. Reproducible from a fixed PRNG seed (2026 0427). Filtering uses an in-memory crossfilter with single-pass, all-other-dimensions aggregation.

Reference

Pattern after the Crossfilter flights demo. Hand-built charts; no chart library.
