Disciplinary Expertise

Curated environments for the discerning estate.

I

Horticultural Curation

Selection, planting, and sustained care of rare and native botanicals, ensuring year-round visual interest and ecological balance tailored to the specific micro-climate of Middle Georgia.

II

Architectural Hardscaping

Precision-engineered stone, retaining walls, and pathway installations that anchor the property. We build permanent outdoor structures that seamlessly integrate with the natural topography.

III

Canopy & Arbor Care

Expert management of legacy trees. Through soil profiling, deep root fertilization, and structural pruning, we preserve the towering assets that define a mature landscape.

IV

Hydrology & Drainage

Advanced grading and subsurface drainage solutions designed to protect architectural foundations while managing water flow elegantly across complex terrains.

V

Bespoke Illumination

Strategic low-voltage lighting design that extends the usability of outdoor spaces, highlights architectural features, and ensures secure, atmospheric evening navigation.

VI

Estate Stewardship

Comprehensive, continuous oversight of the entire landscape ecosystem. We act as the sole custodians of your property’s exterior, managing seasonal transitions with absolute precision.

Studio Philosophy

We do not mow lawns. We steward living architecture.

The modern estate requires a discipline far beyond standard maintenance. It demands a unified approach to soil science, structural engineering, and botanical aesthetics.

Terraverde Ecoscapes was founded on the principle that the exterior of your home is your most expansive living space. Our arborists and landscape architects work in concert to ensure every element—from the oldest oak to the newest stone—appreciates in value and beauty.
