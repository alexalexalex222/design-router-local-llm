# SignalStack — Anchor Principles

- Density over decoration: the hero leads with the actual product surface (a triage console), not a marketing photo or mascot.
- Neutral canvas, restrained signal accent, status chips for the only color that carries meaning.
- Table-first hierarchy: queues, owners, status, and time are scannable before any prose.
- Designed empty/loading/error states — an operations tool lives in those moments.
- Every figure is a bracketed placeholder; no invented metrics, prices, customers, or uptime claims.
- Synthetic, license-safe identity; bespoke inline SVG only, never raster or stock imagery.
