# Orbital Launch Manifest Dark Editorial

Use for technical/aerospace/deep-tech product pages that need a cinematic dark launch narrative, engineering manifest, mission process, operational envelope, staged specs, and high-friction request form without turning into generic SaaS.

## Reusable strengths
- cinematic_hero
- technical_specs
- process_sequence
- form_intake
- dark_contrast

## Pattern rules
- First viewport pairs a massive typographic promise with a sparse technical visual, leaving the product object quiet rather than over-explained.
- Narrative sections move from threshold/mission mood into machine anatomy, capability envelope, orbit/use-case cards, flight sequence, roadmap, and manifest form.
- Specs are shown as constrained labels, tables, and readouts; any unsupplied numbers must remain placeholders or be omitted.
- Responsive behavior collapses complex grids into single-column cards while preserving the section order and dark technical atmosphere.

## Anti-copy
- Do not reuse donor brand names, exact headlines, exact facts, exact product names, exact numbers, or exact visual diagrams.
- Use this example for layout grammar and interaction/state grammar only.
