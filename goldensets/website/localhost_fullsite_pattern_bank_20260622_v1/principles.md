# Localhost Full-Site Pattern Bank — Principles

- Select examples by information architecture and component job, not by donor topic alone.
- Preserve full-site sequencing: hero, proof/context, feature/service body, stateful modules, conversion/final action, and footer.
- The examples intentionally span dense technical, quiet luxury, documentation, product-detail, and single-screen interactive patterns.
- No generated output may claim donor-specific metrics, prices, live values, materials, certifications, or availability unless supplied by the user.
